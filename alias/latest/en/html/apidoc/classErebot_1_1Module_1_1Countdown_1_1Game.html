Constructs a new instance of the Countdown game.

A random (target) number will be selected between $minTarget and $maxTarget. Contestants will be presented with $nbNumbers random numbers to help them reach the target number.

Parameters
int$minTarget(optional) The target number may not be less than this. Defaults to 100.
int$maxTarget(optional) The target number may not be greater than this. Defaults to 999.
int$nbNumbers(optional) How many random numbers will be picked (from $allowedNumbers) to help contestants. Defaults to 7.
list$allowedNumbers(optional) A list with the numbers that may be selected to help contestants. Defaults to: 1-10, 25, 50, 75 & 100.
